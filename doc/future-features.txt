short circuiting mono-morphic generic functions
---
support , (comma) on traits decl within a klass

for example this:
  trait foo;
  trait bar;
could be
  trait foo, bar;
---
support format-va-printf() and format-va-scanf() decorators on methods and
have them propagated through to the generic function definitions.

for example this:
  method format-va-printf(2) object-t va:print-format(object-t self, const char8-t* format, va-list-t args)
would dictate generic functions be generated like:
  format-va-printf(2) object-t va:print-format(object self, const char8-t* format, va-list-t)
and
  format-printf(2) object-t print-format(object self, const char8-t* format, ...)
---
