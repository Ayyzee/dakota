* macro
 * shall have at least one ruleset
 * may have sub-rulesets

* main-ruleset
 * shall have both a lhs and rhs
 * shall scan forward for first token of lhs
 * may have empty lhs
 * may have empty rhs
 * rhs shall be empty when lhs is empty
 * may use macros or sub-rulesets or constraints
 * shall **not** use self recursively

* sub-ruleset (same as ruleset)
 * shall be named
 * may use self recursively

* constraint
 * shall **not** scan forward for first token of lhs
 * may use other constraints
 * shall be compiled
 * in=(sst, range), out=range

* token
 * literal character sequence
